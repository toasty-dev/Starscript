## Overview

**Starscript** is a boosting macro for the game Bee Swarm Simulator on roblox. We have many features that have never been released on a free boost macro along with a sleek UI design. Enjoy premium features like star detection and field boosts.

---

## Features

### ‚≠ê Star Detection
- Detects active Stars.
- Automatically uses configured materials on star.

---

### üìä Tracking & Quality of Life
- Tracks your token uptime depending on configuration to show you what boosts you currently have.
- Improves boost consistency by letting you know exactly what needs to be refreshed.

---

### ‚ú® Auto Field Boost
- Automatically applies **Glitter** to the active field.
- Uses a loaded dice to obtain a full 4x boost. (other dices are able to be used in config)
- Welltimed as to get a full 30 minute boost out of your loaded dice.

---

### ‚ö° AutoBoost
- When you start the macro for a fieldboost, this will automatically use whatever materials you have configured.

---

### üêù Auto Star Saw
- Uses Stingers only when needed to allow for **star saw**, saving stingers and converting your backpack.

---

- Mainly for white hives, this macro will allow you to have your main basically control an alt to make the alt do certain actions upon whatever you have configured.
